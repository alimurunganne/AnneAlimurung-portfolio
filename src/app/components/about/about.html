I began my journey in college with little experience in web development and had no clear idea of what building a full web application entailed. Everything was new — from writing HTML and CSS to understanding how the frontend and backend communicate. I learned step by step, experimenting with small projects and tutorials, which gradually built my confidence and skills. It was a period of curiosity, trial and error, and discovering the basics of programming and design. Each small success motivated me to take on more complex projects.
